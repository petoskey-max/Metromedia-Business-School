FAQs

Metromedia Business School Questions and Answers

Who can apply to Metromedia Business School?
+
Metromedia Business School welcomes working professionals, entrepreneurs, and executives looking to enhance their business skills and leadership capabilities.
Do I need a degree to apply?
+
A recognised bachelor's degree is preferred for most programmes. Professional certificates and substantial work experience may be accepted for executive and short courses, subject to admissions review.
What documents do I need to submit?
+
Typically you'll need a completed application form, a copy of your educational certificates, a professional CV or resume, and a brief statement of purpose.
Is there an interview?
+
Select programmes may require a brief admissions interview. You will be notified after your application is reviewed.
Can I apply if I'm still working?
+
Absolutely. Our programmes are designed with working professionals in mind, offering flexible scheduling to fit your career commitments.
When should I apply?
+
We recommend applying as early as possible. Admissions for the January 2026 cohort are currently ongoing. Visit our website for specific deadline dates.
